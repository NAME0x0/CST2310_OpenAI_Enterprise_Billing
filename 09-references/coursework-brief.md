## Assessment Brief

- Groups of ~4 students
- Choose a case study: a small business information system or part of a larger system
- Our chosen case study: **OpenAI Enterprise Billing System**
- Final submission: **Requirements Specification Report** + **Group Presentation**

### Report Contents

1. **UML Diagrams** — use case, class, activity, sequence diagrams with descriptions
2. **Project plans** — Activity List, Gantt chart, WBS, Risk Matrix
3. **Individually assigned tasks** — each member owns specific deliverables
4. **Meeting notes/minutes** — record of at least 4 formal meetings (weeks 7-12)
